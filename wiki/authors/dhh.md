# David Heinemeier Hansson (DHH)

> Creator of Ruby on Rails, co-founder of 37signals, and outspoken advocate for software aesthetics and developer productivity.

## Background

DHH is known for creating the Ruby on Rails framework and co-founding 37signals (Basecamp, HEY). He is a proponent of "The Rails Way," which emphasizes convention over configuration and developer happiness.

## AI Philosophy

DHH has recently undergone a shift from AI skepticism to being "AI-pilled." He views current LLMs as agents capable of taking on the burden of implementation, allowing developers to focus on high-level design and "taste."

## Core Concepts

- [[aesthetics-is-truth]] — Beauty in code is a proxy for quality.
- [[peak-programmer]] — The end of the era where implementation is the primary value of a coder.
- Small Team Leverage — Using AI to build massive systems with tiny teams.
