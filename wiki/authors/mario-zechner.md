# Mario Zechner

> Engineer and creator of [[pi]], an extensible AI coding agent harness.

## Profile

Mario Zechner is a software engineer known for his work on tools that prioritize minimalism, observability, and human-AI collaboration. He is the primary author of [[pi]], a tool built to address the quality and context management issues found in early AI coding agents.

## Philosophy

Zechner emphasizes "Grey Box Engineering"—the idea that humans must remain intimately familiar with their codebases even when using AI. He warns against the "Ouroboros of Review Agents" and advocates for building "malleable" tools that can be adapted to specific project needs.
