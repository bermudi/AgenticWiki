# Martin Fowler

> Chief Scientist at Thoughtworks and a pioneer of Agile software development and Refactoring.

## Biography

Martin Fowler is a prominent software engineer and author, known for his work on software architecture, refactoring, and the Agile Manifesto. He focuses on patterns of enterprise application architecture and how to design software that is maintainable over the long term.

## AI Perspective

Fowler views AI as a significant shift in the industry, comparable to the rise of the Internet. He emphasizes that the core principles of software engineering—specifically modularity and testing—remain the primary defense against the complexities introduced by AI. He is wary of the "snake oil" industry that surrounds AI, similar to how he viewed the commercialization of Agile.
