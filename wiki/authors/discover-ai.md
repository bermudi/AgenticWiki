# Discover AI

> Discover AI (code4AI) is a YouTube channel creating technical deep-dive videos on RAG, GraphRAG, multi-agent systems, and AI engineering. The channel is known for detailed paper walkthroughs with visual explanations of architectures, algorithms, and benchmark results.
