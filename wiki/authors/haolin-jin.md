# Haolin Jin

> Researcher at University of Sydney; lead author of the systematic overcorrection study in LLM code review.

First author of the study revealing that LLMs systematically misclassify correct code as non-compliant, with false negative rates reaching 88% under detailed prompts. Proposed the Fix-guided Verification Filter as mitigation. Also introduced the FN rationale taxonomy categorizing overcorrection mechanisms.
