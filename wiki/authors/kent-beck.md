# Kent Beck

> The creator of Extreme Programming (XP) and a pioneer of Test-Driven Development (TDD).

## Biography

Kent Beck is one of the most influential figures in modern software engineering. He is the author of several seminal books on TDD, Smalltalk, and software design. He was one of the original signatories of the Agile Manifesto.

## AI Perspective

Beck describes the AI as a "genie" that changes the cost structure of software development. He believes that TDD is more important than ever because it provides the necessary feedback loop to verify the output of AI agents. He emphasizes the shift from "how to implement" to "what to implement," requiring deeper domain knowledge from engineers.
