# Addy Osmani

> Engineer and author who coined the term **[[comprehension-debt]]** — the gap between code that exists in a system and how much any human actually understands.

## Overview

Addy Osmani published an article titled "Comprehension Debt" that named the phenomenon [[the-gray-cat|The Gray Cat]] and others were experiencing but couldn't articulate. His coinage of the term gave engineers the language to discuss the cognitive cost of AI-assisted development.

## Key Contributions

- Coined the term **[[comprehension-debt]]**, which The Gray Cat and others elaborated into a distinction from technical debt
- The core line: "Making code cheap to generate doesn't make understanding cheap to skip"
