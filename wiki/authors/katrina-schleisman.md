# Katrina Schleisman

> Researcher at Galois, Inc. (Minneapolis). Co-author (with [[eric-davis|Eric Davis]]) of an architecture integrating episodic and semantic memory in AI teammates, including the meta-episode (intervention-as-transition) construct and a default-mode-network framing of reflective after-action learning.

## Contributions

- **Episodic + semantic memory for machine teammates** ([[episodic-memory-for-agents]]). With [[eric-davis|Davis]] (first author): an architecture integrating episodic events (spatiotemporal) with a self-organizing-map semantic memory, extended with **meta-episodes** — interventions represented as labeled directed edges between semantic clusters, forming a queryable transition system. The work also articulates the **default-mode-network framing**: today's AI is "mission-focused" and "lays inert" once a task completes; next-generation teammates need a reflective after-action mode that learns during downtime.
