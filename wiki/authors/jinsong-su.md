# Jinsong Su

> Jinsong Su is a professor at Xiamen University and corresponding author of the MemGraphRAG paper (KDD 2026). His research spans NLP, machine translation, and recently GraphRAG with memory-augmented multi-agent systems.

## Affiliation
- Xiamen University, Xiamen, China
- Key Laboratory of Digital Protection and Intelligent Processing of Intangible Cultural Heritage of Fujian and Taiwan, Ministry of Culture and Tourism
- Institute of Artificial Intelligence, Xiamen University
- Email: jssu@xmu.edu.cn

## Key Contributions
- **MemGraphRAG** (KDD 2026): Memory-based multi-agent GraphRAG
- **HippoRAG / HippoRAG2**: Associative memory for GraphRAG (with Qinggang Zhang et al.)
- **When to Use Graphs in RAG** (arXiv 2025): Comprehensive analysis of GraphRAG applicability
- Extensive work on neural machine translation, document-level translation, and multi-hop QA
