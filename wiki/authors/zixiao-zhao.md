# Zixiao Zhao

> Researcher at University of British Columbia; lead author of the Bias in the Loop study auditing LLM-as-a-Judge for software engineering.

Designed and executed the systematic audit of 12 prompt-induced biases in LLM code judges across three tasks (code generation, repair, test generation) and three models. Key contribution: demonstrating that biases act as positional priors with consistent sign-flips depending on candidate order, and that high consistency rates under bias cues create a dangerous illusion of reliability.
