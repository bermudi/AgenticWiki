# Sung Ju Hwang

> Senior / corresponding author of *MemRefine: LLM-Guided Compression for Long-Term Agent Memory* (arXiv 2606.13177, June 2026). Dual affiliation with KAIST and DeepAuto.ai.

## Contribution

Corresponding author of the MemRefine paper. The work formalizes [[storage-budgeted-memory|storage-budgeted memory management]] as a query-agnostic max-min program and introduces [[memrefine|MemRefine]] — a post-construction, LLM-guided compression framework that uses similarity only to propose candidate pairs and defers delete, merge, and preserve decisions to an LLM judge grounded in factual content.
