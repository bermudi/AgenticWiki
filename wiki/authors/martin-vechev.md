# Martin Vechev

> Professor at ETH Zurich (Department of Computer Science) and affiliated with LogicStar.ai. Senior author of the first rigorous evaluation of AGENTS.md files for coding agents.

## Contributions

Vechev was a senior author of the "Evaluating AGENTS.md" study by [[thibaud-gloaguen|Gloaguen et al.]] (2026), which provides the first large-scale empirical evaluation of context files for coding agents. The study introduces AGENTBENCH, a benchmark of 138 instances across 12 repositories with developer-written context files, and evaluates four agent/model pairings across two benchmarks.

The study's evaluation methodology — controlled experiments with measurable outcomes (test pass rates, cost, behavioral traces) — reflects a focus on rigorous empirical assessment of coding agent behavior.
