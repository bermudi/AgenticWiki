# Clankers

> Pejorative term for poorly designed AI agents that generate [[slop]] and lack observability or control.

## Overview

"Clankers" are AI agents that operate with too much autonomy and too little transparency. They often have complex, opaque loops that make it difficult for the human user to understand *why* a certain change was made.

## Characteristics

- **High Friction, Low Reward**: They often fail on simple tasks despite complex internal logic.
- **Context Unawareness**: They tend to pull in too much or too little context, leading to poor code quality.
- **Black Box Nature**: Users cannot easily see or modify the agent's internal prompts or tools.
