# Key points extracted during ingest

- **Vibes vs. Engineering**: LLMs are great at "vibing" (generating plausible-looking code) but often fail on "hard problems" that require deep understanding of a complex codebase.
- **The Context Gap**: Simple RAG is often insufficient for complex codebases. You need graph-based context, cross-repo knowledge, and understanding of implicit dependencies.
- **Correctness over Completion**: Engineering rigor requires verification (tests, linting) rather than just accepting the first output.
- **Cody's Approach**: Uses Sourcegraph's indices to provide high-fidelity context (symbols, references, definitions) to the LLM.
- **Solving Hard Problems**: Requires a loop of: Research -> Propose -> Verify -> Refine.
